FAQ

Intquest?
Intquest is a web application that helps you quickly study computer science concepts for interviews. Users post, answer, and review interview questions sorted by concept.
I already use Stack Overflow / Quora / Google to study. What makes this app different?
Intquest just compiles a shitload of questions. Jumping from site to site to practice interview questions is the worst. You should be able to select the concepts you need to practice and immediately begin prepping.
What types of questions are on Intquest?
Questions you can expect to see in an coding interview. Questions covering concepts from data structures to math to security. Questions ranging from incredibly specific details to incredibly broad design problems. Really pretty much anything.
Who can post questions?
Anyone can post a question- jsut be sure to tag it with the relevant comp sci concepts.
Who can answer questions?
Again, anyone! Your answer doesn't even have to be complete- it can be a link to another website that gives a good explanation/
